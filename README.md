# Overview

This repository contains my published undergraduate research work on the **Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) analysis of hydrogen-fueled scramjet injector geometries** using **ANSYS Fluent**.

The objective of this study was to investigate how different injector (strut) geometries influence the aerodynamic and combustion characteristics inside a supersonic scramjet combustor.

Three injector configurations were analyzed and compared under identical operating conditions to understand their effects on:

- Shock-wave formation
- Fuel-air mixing
- Turbulence generation
- Combustion efficiency
- Pressure recovery
- Overall combustor performance

The work combines principles of **compressible flow**, **gas dynamics**, **combustion**, **CFD**, and **propulsion engineering**.

# Research Objectives

The primary objectives of this project were:

- Study hydrogen fuel injection in a scramjet combustor.
- Compare different injector geometries.
- Investigate shock-wave structures.
- Analyze turbulent mixing of hydrogen and air.
- Evaluate combustion efficiency.
- Study pressure recovery inside the combustor.
- Identify the injector providing the best overall performance.

---

# Injector Geometries Investigated

Three different injector configurations were considered:

### Wedge Strut

- Moderate shock generation
- Stable vortex formation
- Good pressure recovery
- Best overall performance

---

### Lobed Strut

- Strong vortex generation
- Excellent fuel-air mixing
- Higher combustion efficiency
- Increased pressure losses

---

### Diamond Strut

- Lower aerodynamic losses
- Smaller vortices
- Reduced mixing
- Lower combustion efficiency

# Major Findings

The comparative study showed that:

- The **Wedge Strut** provided the best balance between combustion efficiency and pressure recovery.
- The **Lobed Strut** generated stronger vortices, resulting in superior fuel-air mixing but higher total pressure losses.
- The **Diamond Strut** minimized aerodynamic losses but produced weaker mixing, leading to lower combustion efficiency.
- Injector geometry plays a critical role in determining overall scramjet combustor performance.
